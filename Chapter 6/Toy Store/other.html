Events & Workshops

Our Events are held on Tuesdays and Thursdays. Sign ups are suggested before the event, but not required! If you are a busy parent but don't have enough time, send us a message (here) and let us know. We'll put you and your kid(s) on the waitlist, and move on with the enlisting process from there! Plus, there is no sign up fee. When you pay up front, we still offer a refund if our workshop was not satisfactory.
